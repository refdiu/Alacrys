About
Alacrys is an ancient continent, a land brimming with magic and opportunities, a land shaped by the rise and fall of multiple empires and races.. whose ruins now lie layered beneath thriving cities and forgotten wilds. Where the cities are shaped by the times of gruesome war and peace alike, each generation believing itself destined to endure where the last one collapsed. Vast expanses of grasslands, shattered mountain ranges, thriving wilds and sprawling coastlines are home to a multitude of races with individuals, both rich and poor who co-exist through a fragile balance of trade, rivalry and uncertain alliances forged due to necessity, not trust, for the memory of past wars live engraved deeply within their culture and carved along their borders, a lingering weight that refuses to fade. Yet division is a peril none can indulge, for the farthest reaches of the continent lies the Ocean of Thousand Whispers which is both violent and unnatural, beyond which sprawls a distant, corrupted land where the heavens burn in unnatural hues and reality fractures under the strain of distorted transgression. From across those waters comes relentless surges of demonic horrors, that spill forth in writhing chaos. Monstrous hordes, unbound by form or reason and where they pass, the very earth seems to sicken, life withers to dust, stones crumble as though it were decayed bone and the firmament above dims, as if in solemn despair. And in those dismal hours, humanity must unite to face the common foe..
